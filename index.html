Reasoning Method

What is Reasoning?

The ability to think rationally to form sound judgments and defend one's position is referred to as Reasoning. It is concerned, in other words, with recognizing, analyzing, and evaluating arguments. Reasoning is utilized not only to solve immediate difficulties but also to forecast future problems.

Reasoning plays an important role in adjusting to one's surroundings. It affects not just one's cognitive activity but also one's behavior and personality. The process of making decisions, predicting outcomes, or developing explanations based on prior knowledge is also covered by reasoning. To determine how these explanations and information are derived from their source, a Reasoning Method is used.
